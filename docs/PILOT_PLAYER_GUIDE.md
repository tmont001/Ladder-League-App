# Ladder League — Pilot Player Guide

**Audience:** Players joining a league during the pilot.
**Production URL:** `https://YOUR-PRODUCTION-URL-HERE` ← your organizer will send this

---

## 1. Opening the League

1. Open the link your organizer sent you in any web browser.
2. On a phone, open it in **Safari** (iPhone) or **Chrome** (Android).
3. You do not need to create an account or download an app.

---

## 2. Entering Your Access Code

Your organizer will send you a personal **access code** — a short string of letters and numbers.

1. On the app's home screen, enter your access code and click **Join**.
2. You will land on your league's dashboard.
3. Your code stays active in your browser. You do not need to re-enter it unless you clear your browser data or switch devices.

**If your code does not work:**
- Check for typos — codes are case-sensitive.
- Make sure you are at the correct URL.
- Contact your organizer to confirm you have the right code.

---

## 3. Viewing Standings and Schedule

Once inside the league:

- **Standings tab** — your current ranking, record, and how other players rank.
- **Schedule tab** — all matches, including upcoming, past, and your results.

Tabs may be named differently in a Ladder league (challenges appear instead of a fixed schedule).

---

## 4. Submitting a Score

After you have played a match:

1. Find the match in the **Schedule** tab.
2. Click **Submit Score**.
3. Enter the score for each set and click **Submit**.
4. Your opponent will receive a notification to confirm or dispute.

---

## 5. Confirming or Disputing an Opponent's Score

When your opponent submits a score, you will see a notification (bell icon at the top of the screen).

1. Open the notification or find the match in the Schedule tab.
2. Review the score.
3. Click **Confirm** if the score is correct — it will be recorded.
4. Click **Dispute** if the score is wrong — your organizer will review and enter the official result.

---

## 6. Ladder Challenges (Ladder Leagues Only)

In a Ladder league, you move up by challenging players ranked above you.

**Issuing a challenge:**
1. Go to the **Challenges** tab.
2. Click **Issue Challenge**.
3. Select your opponent. You can only challenge players within the number of spots above you that your organizer has set (for example, up to 2 spots above).
4. Confirm. Your opponent receives a notification.

**Accepting or declining a challenge:**
1. Check your notifications (bell icon).
2. Open the challenge.
3. Click **Accept** to play or **Decline** to turn it down.
4. If you accept, the match will appear in the schedule once arrangements are made.

**After your Ladder match is played:**
- Submit the score the same way as any other match.
- Your position on the ladder updates based on the result.

---

## 7. Why Organizers Use Magic Links but Players Use Access Codes

Organizers manage the whole league and need a secure, verified login. They use a **Magic Link** — a one-time email link — for that level of access.

Players only need access to their own league view. An **access code** is simpler and does not require you to create an account or manage a password.

---

## 8. Protecting Your Access Code

- Your code is your key to the league. Do not share it with anyone else.
- If someone else enters your code, they will see your match history and can submit scores on your behalf.
- If you believe your code has been compromised, tell your organizer immediately so a new one can be issued.

---

## 9. Mobile Tips

- The app works in any modern mobile browser — no download needed.
- If the app looks misaligned or frozen, refresh the page.
- On iPhone: tap the Share button → **Add to Home Screen** for a shortcut.
- On Android: tap the browser menu → **Add to Home Screen**.

---

## 10. Getting Help

If something is not working:

- Refresh the page and try again.
- Try opening the link in a different browser.
- If you cannot join with your code, contact your organizer — they can look up your code and resend the invite.
- If the match result is wrong after a dispute, your organizer will correct it.
